Woah... Déjà Vu.

jmreedy is the online persona of a guy named Jason who enjoys making websites for a living. Currently, he finds himself building out a design system and component library for the folks over at Total Expert. In a previous life, he lead design and front-end development for MarketWatch; making it a more beautiful and engaging place to gather financial news.

Whilst not writing code or drawing pictures in Figma, he enjoys building virtual pinball machines in-between chasing after his three energetic children.

The Elevator Pitch

Past experiences would suggest that designers usually aren't capable of supporting development efforts once merge requests take flight. Meanwhile, engineers don't often find themselves with extra time to obsess over type scales and font weights. I've built my career off being the expection to both; finding solace between the two specialties with a proven track record of bridging the gap.

  • Design Debonair

    Your customers deserve beautiful and well thought out experiences. Be it a niche SaaS application or a major financial media outlet that reaches millions, in my 15+ years designing for the web, I’ve done it all.

  • Markup Magician

    I enjoy and take pride in my ability to write beautifully scemantic, production ready HTML & CSS. This frees up your full-stack engineers to do the work that they actually want to do.

  • Figma Fanboy

    I obsess over learning everything there is to know about the latest enhancements regarding how to increase productivity inside every designer's new favorite daily driver.

  • Systems Savant

    Nothing will kill productivity more than your ICs reinventing the wheel with each new project. Design systems help to alleviate that

  • Accessibility Advocate

    ADA compliance should be top of mind for any SaaS organization and for good reason. A website that works for all
